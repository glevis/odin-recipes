Shio

Description

Shio is a salty ramen packed with umami.

Ingredients

  • 350 mL All Purpose Chintan
  • 30 mL White Wine Chicken Shio Tare
  • 15 mL All Purpose Negi (Scallion) Oil
  • 135g Tokyo Style Noodle

Steps

  1. While cooking the noodles, add the tare, aroma oil, and soup to the serving bowl.
  2. Strain the noodles.
  3. Fold the noodles into the soup.
  4. Add any toppings.
