Tori Paitan

Description

Tori Paitan is a chicken-bone based broth with a light but rich taste

Ingredients

  • 300 mL Chicken Paitan Soup
  • 30 mL Light Shoyu Tare or Soy/Shio Blend Tare
  • 15 mL Chicken and Scallion Oil or Chicken Fat
  • 130g Makeshift-Hakata Style Noodle

Steps

  1. Add chicken parts and water to a pot.
  2. Cook at a medium-boil for 6 hours.
  3. Add aromatics, boil thoroughly for 1 hour.
  4. Strain the soup.
  5. Emulsify the soup.
  6. Combine with Tare, Oil, and Noodles.
