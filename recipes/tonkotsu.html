Tonkotsu

Description

Tonkotsu is a pork-bone based ramen, creamy in color and taste.

Ingredients

  • 300 mL Tonkotsu Soup
  • 30 mL Light Shoyu Tare or Barebones Shio Tare
  • 15 mL All Purpose Negi (Scallion) Oil
  • 130g Makeshift-Hakata Style Noodle

Steps

  1. While cooking the noodles, add the tare, aroma oil, and soup to the serving bowl.
  2. Strain the noodles.
  3. Fold the noodles into the soup.
  4. Add any toppings.
